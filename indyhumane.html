Questions answered

You have questions. We have answers.

How is my monthly gift used?
Every dollar goes directly to animal care at IndyHumane, including daily food, medical treatment, spay and neuter surgeries, behavioral enrichment, and staffing. IndyHumane receives no government funding, so your monthly gift is the engine that keeps everything running.
Can I cancel my monthly gift at any time?
Yes. You can cancel or adjust your monthly gift at any time with no questions asked and no fees. Just contact us at donate@indyhumane.org or call us directly. We make it easy.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. IndyHumane is a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit organization (EIN: 35-0876385). Your monthly gifts are fully tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. You will receive an annual giving statement for your records.
What makes IndyHumane different from other shelters?
IndyHumane does not euthanize animals for length of stay, cost of care, or space. Every animal receives individualized medical and behavioral care. We also operate one of Indiana's first dog parks, a low-cost spay and neuter clinic, and a pet food pantry serving families who need help keeping their pets. We are a full-service community resource, not just a shelter.
What is the Paw Pack monthly giving community?
The Paw Pack is IndyHumane's official monthly giving community. Members at three tiers — Shelter Friend, Pack Leader, and Guardian — receive exclusive updates, behind-the-scenes access, and perks that deepen their connection to the animals they are helping. It's more than a donation. It's a membership in something meaningful.
How will I know my gift is making a difference?
Every month, Paw Pack members receive a real impact update with stories about specific animals their support helped. Pack Leader and Guardian members also receive quarterly photos of shelter animals. You will always know where your money went.
Does IndyHumane receive government funding?
No. IndyHumane is a private 501(c)3 nonprofit and relies entirely on the generosity of donors and community partners. This is why monthly giving is so critical. It creates the stable, predictable funding we need to care for thousands of animals year-round.
Can I give in honor or memory of a pet?
Absolutely. IndyHumane offers gifts in memory or in honor of a loved one or a beloved pet. Visit our Ways to Give page for details on how to set this up for a one-time or recurring gift.
How do I volunteer or foster in addition to donating?
We would love to have you! Visit indyhumane.org/volunteer to apply for our shelter volunteer program, or indyhumane.org/foster to learn about opening your home to a pet in need. Volunteering and fostering are free, meaningful ways to make an even bigger difference alongside your monthly gift.
What if I want to give more than $100 a month?
We love that question. For larger recurring gifts, corporate partnerships, or planned giving, please reach out to our development team directly at indyhumane.org/contact. We have a range of major giving and legacy options available for donors who want to make an even greater impact.
